

<p><h2>Proxy,</h2></p>
     
<p>This is the most renowned aspect of the framework. it enables interception / modification of requests and responses while interacting w/ applications.</p>

<p><h2>Repeater,</h2></p>

<p>This allows for capturing, modifying, and resending the saem request multiple times.<br>
This proves useful when crafting payloads through trial and error.</p>

<p><h2>Intruder,</h2></p>

<p>Allows for spraying endpoints with requests(rate-limited in communtity), common for brute-forcing and fuzzing endpoints.</p>

<p><h2>Decoder,</h2></p>

<p>Decodes captured information or encodes payloads before sending them to the target.</p>

<p><h2>Comparer,</h2></p>

<p>Compares two pieces of data at either the word or byte level.</p>

<p><h2>Sequencer,</h2></p>

<p>Deployed when assessing the randomness of tokens, such as a session cookie or other "Random" data.</p>
